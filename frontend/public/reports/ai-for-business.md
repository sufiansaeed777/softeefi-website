## ğŸ’¡ Practical Prompt Engineering {#prompt-engineering}

### The Formula That Works

**Bad Prompt:** "Write about my business"

**Good Prompt:** "Write a 200-word LinkedIn post about how our Manchester-based accounting firm helps small businesses save 20% on taxes through R&D credits. Use a professional but friendly tone."

### The Perfect Prompt Structure:

1. **Role:** "You are a [expert type]"
2. **Context:** "We are a [business description]"
3. **Task:** "Create/Write/Analyze [specific output]"
4. **Format:** "In [X words/bullets/paragraphs]"
5. **Tone:** "Using [professional/casual/friendly] tone"

### Examples That Get Results:

```
"You are a customer service expert. A customer ordered a laptop 
last week but hasn't received it due to shipping delays. Write 
a empathetic email response that apologizes, explains the delay, 
offers a 10% discount, and provides tracking information. 
Keep it under 150 words and use a warm, professional tone."
```
